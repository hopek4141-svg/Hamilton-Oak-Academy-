About Us

Hamilton Oak Academy is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing exceptional secondary and sixth form education. Our commitment to academic excellence, personal development, and community engagement sets us apart.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower students to achieve their full potential through high-quality education, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong passion for learning.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence, creating confident, capable, and compassionate individuals prepared for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Programs

Secondary Curriculum

Our comprehensive secondary curriculum covers:

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • Modern Languages
  • History & Geography
  • Physical Education
  • Arts & Design
  • Technology & Computing

Preparing students for GCSEs and beyond with expert instruction and personalized support.

Sixth Form Courses

Advanced A-Level and vocational programs including:

  • A-Level Mathematics & Further Mathematics
  • A-Level Sciences
  • A-Level English & Humanities
  • Business & Economics
  • Foreign Languages
  • Creative & Performing Arts
  • BTec Qualifications
  • UCAS Preparation

Pathways to university and professional careers with dedicated mentorship.
